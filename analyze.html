Quantitative Aptitude

Pattern: High weightage on Arithmetic. Calculations can be lengthy.

The Ratio Hack: Avoid 'X' variables for Ages, Mixtures, and Boats. Use Ratio-shifting or check options directly.

Core Priorities:

  • Arithmetic: SI/CI, Time & Work, Speed & Distance.
  • Advanced: Expect 2 Mensuration & 1-2 Trig Qs.
  • Calculations: Squares (1-30) & Cubes (1-20) are mandatory.

Reasoning (IPT)

Pattern: Focus on Depiction & Interpretation (Logic recognition).

Alphanumeric Hack: Skip the 4-line instruction block. Scan the condition (e.g. Symbol-Letter-Symbol) and search immediately.

Critical Types:

  • Alphanumeric Series: Counting & condition checks.
  • 3-Digit Numbers: Digit operations on multiple numbers.
  • Alphabet Shifting: Vowel/Consonant transformation rules.
17 Questions

English Language

Pattern: Contextual Grammar & Reading Comprehension speed.

RC Keyword Anchor: Read the questions before the passage to identify specific search keywords.

Strategic Anchors:

  • Jumbles: Identify the Noun (Subject) first. Avoid pronoun starts.
  • Transitions: "Therefore/However" are conclusion indicators.
  • Grammar: Focus on Subject-Verb Agreement & Conditionals.

Final Momentum Guide

HPCL provides ~52 seconds per question. Speed is your primary weapon. If a calculation takes more than 45 seconds, mark it, skip it, and return later. With no negative marks, ensure every bubble is filled in the final 5 minutes.
